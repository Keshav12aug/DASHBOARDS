
# Amazon Prime Video Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Amazon Prime Video dashboard developed to analyze and visualize trends across movies and TV shows available on the platform. The dashboard provides insights into ratings, genres, release years, countries, and content distribution.

The purpose of this project is to transform raw streaming platform data into meaningful business insights using data visualization techniques.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze the distribution of Movies and TV Shows
- Identify the most popular genres and ratings
- Study content growth over time
- Explore country-wise content availability
- Generate business insights from streaming data

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Rating by Total Shows
Displays the number of shows available under various audience ratings.

Key categories:
- 13+
- 16+
- ALL
- 18+
- PG-13
- R

Insight:
Most content belongs to the 13+ and 16+ categories, indicating strong focus on family and general audience entertainment.

---

### 2. Genres by Total Shows
Visualizes the most common genres available on the platform.

Top genres include:
- Drama
- Comedy
- Drama, Suspense
- Comedy, Drama
- Documentary

Insight:
Drama and Comedy dominate the platform’s content library.

---

### 3. Movies vs TV Shows
Donut chart comparing content type distribution.

| Type | Percentage |
|---|---|
| Movies | 80.82% |
| TV Shows | 19.18% |

Insight:
Amazon Prime Video contains significantly more movies than TV shows.

---

### 4. Total Shows by Release Year
Line chart showing yearly content growth.

Insight:
Content production increased rapidly after 2000, especially during the streaming boom period.

---

### 5. Total Shows by Country
Map visualization representing geographical content distribution.

Insight:
The United States and India contribute heavily to the platform's content catalog.

## Business Insights

- Drama is the most dominant content genre.
- Movies form the majority of the platform’s content.
- Streaming content production has grown significantly in recent years.
- Family-friendly content categories are highly prevalent.
- Content distribution is concentrated among major countries.

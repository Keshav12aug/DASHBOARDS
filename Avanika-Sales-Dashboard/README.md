# Avanika Store Annual Sales Dashboard 2022

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive sales dashboard for Avanika Store created using Excel. The dashboard analyzes annual sales performance, customer demographics, order trends, sales channels, and regional performance for the year 2022.

The dashboard helps identify key business trends and supports data-driven decision-making through visual analytics.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze monthly sales and order trends
- Compare sales contribution by gender
- Study order status distribution
- Identify top-performing states
- Understand customer demographics
- Evaluate sales channels performance
- Generate strategic business recommendations

---

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Orders vs Sales Analysis
A combined chart comparing monthly sales amount and total order count.

Insight:
Sales peaked during the first quarter and gradually declined toward the end of the year.

---

### 2. Sales Distribution: Men vs Women
Pie chart showing gender-wise sales contribution.

| Gender | Contribution |
|---|---|
| Women | 64% |
| Men | 36% |

Insight:
Women contribute the majority of total sales.

---

### 3. Order Status Analysis
Pie chart representing delivery and cancellation status.

| Status | Percentage |
|---|---|
| Delivered | 92% |
| Cancelled | 3% |
| Returned | 3% |
| Refunded | 2% |

Insight:
The store maintains a high successful delivery rate.

---

### 4. Top 5 States by Sales
Bar chart displaying highest revenue-generating states.

Top states:
- Maharashtra
- Karnataka
- Uttar Pradesh
- Telangana
- Tamil Nadu

Insight:
Maharashtra generates the highest sales revenue.

---

### 5. Orders by Age and Gender
Clustered chart comparing customer age groups and gender.

Age categories:
- Adult
- Teenager
- Senior

Insight:
Women aged 30–49 contribute significantly to total orders.

---

### 6. Orders by Sales Channel
Pie chart showing platform-wise order distribution.

Top channels:
- Amazon
- Myntra
- Flipkart
- Meesho
- Ajio

Insight:
Amazon is the highest-performing sales channel.

---

## Key Insights

- Women contribute approximately 65% of total sales.
- Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top-performing states.
- Adults aged 30–49 generate nearly 50% of total orders.
- Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra are the most effective sales platforms.
- Delivery success rate is exceptionally high at 92%.

---

## Strategic Recommendation

Focus marketing campaigns on women aged 30–49 in high-performing states using Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra for maximum conversion and revenue growth.
